About Scenarios

Success Scenario: Simulates a healthy deployment with 99% success rate and low latency. The AI analysis will approve the rollout and traffic will gradually shift to the new version.

Failure Scenario: Simulates a problematic deployment with 70% success rate and high latency. The AI analysis will detect issues and recommend rollback to prevent impact.

To switch scenarios: Update the SCENARIO_MODE environment variable in the rollout configuration and trigger a new deployment.
